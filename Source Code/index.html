Ignite Your Potential at USNStart - Where Vision Meets Opportunity

Welcome to USNStart, the premier coworking space situated on the Bø campus of the University of South-Eastern Norway. We at USNStart offer a dynamic,
contemporary setting that encourages innovation, teamwork, and development.
Whether you're a freelancer, an aspiring business owner, or a member of a vibrant team, our space is designed to meet your needs.

Empowering Innovation and Collaboration:

USNStart offers a range of flexible workspace solutions from hot desks in our open workspace to private offices and state-of-the-art meeting rooms.
With over 500 square meters of thoughtfully designed space, we cater to individuals and groups seeking a supportive community to bring their projects to life.

Designed for Connectivity and Comfort:

Our facility is equipped with high-speed internet, advanced technological resources, and comfortable workstations.
You'll also find leisure and relaxation areas, including a game room and a well-stocked kitchen and dining area, perfect for taking well-deserved breaks.

Accessible and Welcoming:

Situated at the heart of the University, USNStart is not just a workspace but a hub of entrepreneurial spirit. We are home to students, local business professionals,
and industry leaders, creating a unique ecosystem where knowledge meets experience. Join USNStart today and transform your workday into an inspiring journey of progress and productivity.
