About Us

Welcome to USNStart, a cornerstone of innovation nestled in the heart of the University of South-Eastern Norway's Bø campus.
Launched in 2024, our coworking space was born from the desire to create a thriving community for entrepreneurs, students, and professionals.
With the generous support of the university and partnerships with key industry leaders, USNStart has transformed traditional work environments into dynamic spaces that foster creativity and collaboration.

Mission

Our mission at USNStart is to empower individuals and teams to achieve their entrepreneurial aspirations by providing a state-of-the-art coworking environment
that stimulates innovation and growth. We are committed to fostering a culture of openness and collaboration where every member has the opportunity to excel.

Vision

Our vision is to be a leading hub of entrepreneurial activity, recognized for cultivating successful individuals and businesses.
We aim to support the economic growth of our region by attracting talented individuals and fostering connections between academia and the business community.

What Makes USNStart Unique?

  • Community and Networking: We pride ourselves on building a community that values collaboration over competition.
    Our regular networking events, workshops, and speaker sessions provide members with the opportunity to learn, share, and connect with like-minded individuals.
  • Flexible Workspace Solutions: From open-plan desks to private offices and advanced meeting rooms, our facilities are designed to
    accommodate all working styles and team sizes. We offer flexibility with various membership options to suit different needs and goals.
  • Technology and Support: Our members enjoy access to high-speed internet, modern AV equipment, and smart technologies that enhance
    productivity and streamline work processes. In addition, our on-site support staff is always ready to assist with any needs.
  • Location and Accessibility: Situated at the vibrant Bø campus of the University of South-Eastern Norway, USNStart is conveniently located with easy access to public transport,
    dining, and recreational facilities. This prime location not only provides a scenic and stimulating environment but also fosters collaborations with university departments and local businesses.
  • Sustainability and Responsibility: We are committed to sustainability and integrate eco-friendly practices in our operations,
    including energy-efficient utilities, waste reduction programs, and support for sustainable local businesses.

Join Us

Whether you are taking the first steps in your entrepreneurial journey or looking to expand your horizons, USNStart offers a platform where your ambitions
can soar. Explore our membership options and join a community that supports your professional growth and personal well-being.
